# Product Requirements Document: Bharat Ek Rekhoj (India Explorer)

## Overview
Bharat Ek Rekhoj is a comprehensive travel and cultural exploration platform that helps people discover the diverse heritage, destinations, and experiences across India.

## Vision
Create the most comprehensive digital companion for exploring India's rich cultural heritage, tourist destinations, and local experiences, making travel planning effortless and authentic.

## Target Audience
- Domestic travelers in India
- International tourists visiting India
- Cultural enthusiasts
- Heritage explorers
- Adventure travelers
- Family vacation planners

## Core Features

### 1. Destination Discovery
- AI-powered destination recommendations
- Cultural and historical site information
- Hidden gems and offbeat locations
- Seasonal travel suggestions
- Festival and event calendar

### 2. Trip Planning
- Customized itinerary creation
- Budget planning tools
- Transportation options
- Accommodation recommendations
- Local guide connections

### 3. Cultural Insights
- Historical context and stories
- Local customs and etiquette
- Language guides and phrases
- Traditional cuisine recommendations
- Shopping and handicraft guides

### 4. Community Features
- Traveler reviews and experiences
- Photo sharing and storytelling
- Travel buddy matching
- Local expert Q&A
- Safety tips and alerts

## Technical Stack
- Frontend: React + TypeScript
- Backend: Node.js + Express
- Database: PostgreSQL with geospatial extensions
- AI: GPT-4 for recommendations and content
- Maps: Google Maps API, Mapbox
- Content: Multi-language support (20+ Indian languages)

## Success Metrics
- Active users
- Trips planned
- Destinations explored
- User-generated content
- Partner bookings
- User satisfaction

## Timeline
- Phase 1 (MVP): 4 months
- Phase 2 (Community features): 2 months
- Phase 3 (Partner integrations): 3 months

## Competitive Advantage
- India-focused expertise
- Cultural depth
- Multi-language support
- AI-powered personalization
- Community-driven content
