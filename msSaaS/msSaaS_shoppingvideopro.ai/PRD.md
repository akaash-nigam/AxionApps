# Product Requirements Document: Shopping Video Pro

## Overview
Shopping Video Pro is an AI-powered platform that creates professional shopping videos and product demonstrations for e-commerce businesses.

## Vision
Enable every e-commerce seller to create engaging, professional-quality product videos that drive conversions without expensive video production resources.

## Target Audience
- E-commerce store owners
- Amazon and marketplace sellers
- Social commerce creators
- Dropshipping businesses
- Product marketers
- Digital marketing agencies

## Core Features

### 1. AI Video Generation
- Text-to-video product demonstrations
- Automated script generation
- AI voiceover in multiple languages
- Product feature highlighting
- Multiple video style templates

### 2. Product Showcase
- 360-degree product rotation
- Feature callouts and annotations
- Before/after comparisons
- Size and scale demonstrations
- Use case scenarios

### 3. Social Commerce
- Platform-specific formats (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts)
- Trending sound integration
- Hashtag recommendations
- Captions and subtitles
- Thumbnail generation

### 4. Video Optimization
- A/B testing variants
- Performance analytics
- Engagement metrics
- Conversion tracking
- SEO optimization for video

## Technical Stack
- Frontend: React + TypeScript
- Backend: Node.js + Express
- Database: PostgreSQL
- AI: GPT-4 for scripts, Stable Diffusion for visuals, Text-to-Speech
- Video: FFmpeg, WebGL for 3D rendering

## Success Metrics
- Videos created per day
- User retention rate
- Video conversion rates
- Platform integrations
- Revenue per user

## Timeline
- Phase 1 (MVP): 4 months
- Phase 2 (Social features): 2 months
- Phase 3 (Analytics): 2 months

## Competitive Advantage
- AI-powered automation
- Multi-platform optimization
- Fast video generation
- No video editing skills required
