# Product Requirements Document: Interiors AI

## Overview
Interiors AI is an AI-powered interior design assistant specifically tailored for the Indian market, providing culturally relevant design solutions and local product recommendations.

## Vision
Make professional interior design accessible to Indian homeowners and businesses through AI technology that understands local aesthetics, space constraints, and budget considerations.

## Target Audience
- Indian homeowners
- Real estate developers in India
- Interior design firms
- Architects and builders
- Property management companies
- Furniture retailers

## Core Features

### 1. AI-Powered Design
- Indian home layout optimization
- Regional style preferences (Traditional, Contemporary, Fusion)
- Vastu-compliant designs
- Space-saving solutions for compact homes
- Climate-appropriate recommendations

### 2. Local Product Integration
- Integration with Indian furniture marketplaces
- Local vendor recommendations
- Budget-friendly alternatives
- Material availability in local markets
- Installation service provider network

### 3. Cultural Design Elements
- Traditional Indian motifs and patterns
- Festival and occasion-specific decor
- Multi-generational living spaces
- Prayer room and pooja mandir designs
- Indian kitchen and dining layouts

### 4. Budget Planning
- INR-based cost estimation
- Phased implementation plans
- DIY vs professional recommendations
- ROI for property value
- Financing options

## Technical Stack
- Frontend: React + TypeScript
- Backend: Node.js + Express
- Database: PostgreSQL
- AI: GPT-4 for recommendations, Stable Diffusion for visualizations
- Payments: Razorpay, Paytm integration
- Localization: Multi-language support (Hindi, English, regional languages)

## Success Metrics
- User registrations
- Designs created
- Vendor partnerships
- Revenue from marketplace
- Customer satisfaction

## Timeline
- Phase 1 (MVP): 4 months
- Phase 2 (Vendor integration): 2 months
- Phase 3 (Regional expansion): 3 months
