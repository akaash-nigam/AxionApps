### Why does this need to be a spatial app vs traditional 2D?
Traditional supply chain systems force managers to mentally reconstruct complex global networks from fragmented spreadsheets and dashboards. Critical relationships between suppliers, inventory, and transportation remain invisible until problems occur. In spatial computing, the entire supply chain becomes a living 3D network where connections are visible, disruptions are immediately apparent, and optimization opportunities emerge naturally.
