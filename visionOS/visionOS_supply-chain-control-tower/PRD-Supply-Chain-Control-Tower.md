# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Supply Chain Control Tower

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

The Supply Chain Control Tower transforms global supply chain management into an immersive 3D command center where logistics professionals navigate through living supply networks, predict disruptions before they occur, and orchestrate complex global operations with natural gestures. This spatial platform enables supply chain teams to literally see goods flowing across the world, identify bottlenecks as physical constraints, and leverage AI to autonomously optimize routes, inventory, and resources in real-time.

### Strategic Vision
* **Business Transformation**: From reactive firefighting to proactive orchestration
* **Competitive Advantage**: 360° supply chain visibility with predictive intelligence
* **AI Integration**: Autonomous optimization with human strategic oversight
* **Platform Strategy**: Become the global nervous system for supply chain operations
