# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Spatial ERP

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

Spatial ERP reimagines enterprise resource planning as an immersive 3D command center where business operations come alive through spatial visualization and natural interaction. This revolutionary platform transforms abstract financial data, inventory levels, and production schedules into tangible spatial experiences where executives and operators can literally walk through their business processes, manipulate resources with gestures, and orchestrate complex operations through AI-powered spatial interfaces.
