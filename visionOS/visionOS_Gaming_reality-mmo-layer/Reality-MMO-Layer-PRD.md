# Reality MMO Layer - Product Requirements Document
*Spatial Gaming in the AI Era*

---

## Section 1: Game Concept & Abstract

Reality MMO Layer creates a persistent augmented reality MMO that overlays the entire real world with fantasy elements, allowing millions of players to explore, quest, and interact in shared augmented spaces. Using Vision Pro's advanced spatial mapping and GPS integration, players discover virtual dungeons in real buildings, encounter other players as they explore their actual neighborhoods, and participate in a living, breathing virtual world that exists everywhere around us, fundamentally transforming how humans interact with both digital content and physical reality.

### Core Elements
* **Genre & Setting**: Persistent world MMO with real-world integration and fantasy augmentation
* **Unique Spatial Hook**: Entire planet becomes game world with location-based content and territorial control
* **AI Integration**: Dynamic content generation, intelligent NPC behavior, adaptive world events, personalized quest systems
* **Target Platform**: Leverages Vision Pro's spatial mapping, GPS integration, and seamless outdoor-indoor transitions

## Section 2: Business Objective & Market Opportunity

### Market Analysis

1. **What player need does this fulfill?**
   - Next-generation MMO experience that integrates with real-world exploration and social interaction
   - Persistent virtual world that enhances rather than replaces physical reality
   - Global community building through shared augmented experiences in local environments

2. **Who is the target audience?**
   - Primary: MMO gamers ages 16-40 (existing MMO players, mobile gamers, social gamers)
   - Secondary: AR enthusiasts ages 18-45 (technology adopters, fitness gamers, social media users)
   - Tertiary: Community builders ages 20-60 (neighborhood organizations, business owners, tourism industry)

3. **How large is the spatial gaming market?**
   - TAM: $12.8B MMO gaming market + $3.2B location-based gaming + $2.8B AR market
   - SAM: $2.1B next-generation MMO experiences + $850M AR gaming early adopters
   - SOM: $180M premium spatial MMO and location-based gaming pioneers

4. **What competing experiences exist?**
   - Traditional MMOs (screen-bound, isolated from physical world, no spatial interaction)
   - Location-based mobile games (limited AR integration, simple mechanics, screen-dependent)
   - VR MMOs (completely virtual, requires dedicated play space, no real-world integration)

5. **Why are we uniquely positioned?**
   - First true persistent AR MMO with global real-world integration
   - Proprietary spatial mapping technology for seamless indoor-outdoor transitions
   - Expertise in massive multiplayer infrastructure and location-based content systems

6. **Why is now the right time?**
   - MMO market seeking next-generation experiences beyond traditional boundaries
   - Location-based gaming proven successful with 1B+ players in Pokémon GO ecosystem
   - Vision Pro enabling seamless real-virtual integration without handheld devices

7. **How will we reach players?**
   - Partnership with existing MMO communities and guild organizations
   - Location-based marketing in high-traffic urban areas and gaming events
   - Influencer campaigns with MMO streamers and AR content creators

8. **What's our monetization model?**
   - Premium subscription ($19.99/month) with enhanced progression and exclusive content
   - Freemium in-game economy (cosmetics, convenience, guild features)
   - Location partnerships with businesses, tourism, and educational institutions

9. **What defines success?**
   - 1M registered users year 1, 250K daily active users in major metropolitan areas
   - $50M annual revenue through subscriptions and in-game economy

10. **Go/No-Go recommendation?**
    - **GO** - Unprecedented market opportunity, proven location-based gaming demand, first-mover advantage
