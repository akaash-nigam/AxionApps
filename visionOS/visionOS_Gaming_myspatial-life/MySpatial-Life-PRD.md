## Section 1: Game Concept & Abstract

MySpatial Life transforms your living space into a persistent life simulation where digital family members live alongside you, pursue their own dreams and relationships, and create emergent stories that unfold whether you're watching or not. Players guide multiple generations through careers, romance, and life challenges while AI-driven characters develop genuine personalities, form unpredictable relationships, remember shared experiences, and age realistically over months and years. This revolutionary life sim proves that spatial computing can create emotional connections deeper than any screen-based game by making virtual lives feel genuinely present in your world.

### Core Elements
* **Genre & Setting**: Life simulation in contemporary/near-future settings within your actual home
* **Unique Spatial Hook**: Characters inhabit your real space, using furniture, having favorite spots
* **AI Integration**: Advanced personality AI, emergent behaviors, memory systems, relationship dynamics
* **Target Platform**: Vision Pro's persistent spatial anchors, long-term tracking, always-on capabilities
