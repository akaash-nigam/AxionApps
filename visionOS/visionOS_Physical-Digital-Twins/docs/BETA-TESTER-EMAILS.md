## Email Best Practices

### General Tips

1. **Keep it Personal**
   - Use recipient's first name
   - Reference previous conversations
   - Keep tone friendly and casual

2. **Be Clear and Concise**
   - Use bullet points and formatting
   - Bold important information
   - Include clear calls-to-action

3. **Provide Context**
   - Explain why you're emailing
   - Set expectations for response time
   - Link to relevant resources

4. **Show Appreciation**
   - Thank testers frequently
   - Acknowledge their time and effort
   - Share how their feedback helped

### Timing

- **Recruitment:** Send on weekday mornings (Tue-Thu, 9-11 AM)
- **Invitations:** Send within 24 hours of agreement
- **Updates:** Send when major builds are ready
- **Feedback Requests:** Wait 5-7 days after invitation
- **Re-engagement:** Wait 2 weeks of inactivity

### Response Management

- **24-Hour Rule:** Respond to bug reports within 24 hours
- **Set Expectations:** Tell testers when to expect fixes
- **Follow Through:** Always close the loop on reported issues
- **Be Transparent:** Share what you can/can't implement and why
