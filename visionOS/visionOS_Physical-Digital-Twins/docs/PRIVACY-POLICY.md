# Privacy Policy for Physical-Digital Twins

**Effective Date:** December 1, 2024
**Last Updated:** December 1, 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to Physical-Digital Twins ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

**Physical-Digital Twins** is a visionOS application designed to help you catalog and organize your physical possessions by creating digital twins. The app is designed with privacy as a core principle.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

When you use Physical-Digital Twins, you may provide the following information:

1. **Inventory Data**
   - Item titles, descriptions, and metadata
   - Purchase information (price, date, location)
   - Custom notes and location tags
   - Categories and organization preferences

2. **Photos**
   - Photos you take or select from your photo library
   - Photos are stored locally on your device only

3. **Barcode Scan Data**
   - Barcode numbers (ISBN, UPC, EAN, QR codes)
   - Used to fetch product information from external APIs
   - Not stored permanently; used only for API lookups

### Information Collected Automatically

1. **Device Information**
   - Device model (Apple Vision Pro)
   - Operating system version (visionOS)
   - App version number
   - Anonymous usage statistics (if enabled)

2. **Usage Data**
   - Feature usage patterns (which features you use most)
   - Performance metrics (app crashes, load times)
   - Error logs for debugging purposes

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we **do not** collect:

- ❌ We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
- ❌ We do not track your location via GPS
- ❌ We do not access your contacts, calendar, or messages
- ❌ We do not collect payment information (if app is free/paid via App Store)
- ❌ We do not share your data with third parties for advertising
- ❌ We do not sell your data to anyone

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

### Primary Functions

1. **Inventory Management**
   - Store and organize your inventory items locally on your device
   - Display your items, photos, and metadata in the app
   - Enable search, filter, and sort functionality

2. **Product Information Enrichment**
   - Send barcode numbers to Google Books API to fetch book metadata
   - Retrieve product details (title, author, description, cover images)
   - Enhance your inventory with accurate product information

3. **App Improvement**
   - Analyze anonymous usage patterns to improve features
   - Identify and fix bugs through crash reports
   - Optimize performance and user experience

### Data Storage

All your personal inventory data is stored **locally on your device** using:
- **Core Data** for structured item information
- **File System** for photos (stored in app's Documents directory)

**We do not upload your inventory data to our servers.** Everything stays on your device.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Google Books API

- **Purpose:** Fetch book metadata when you scan ISBN barcodes
- **Data Shared:** ISBN/barcode number only
- **Privacy Policy:** [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Data Storage:** Google does not store your queries permanently

### Apple Services

- **iCloud (Optional):** If you enable iCloud backup, your app data may be backed up to iCloud according to Apple's privacy policy
- **Privacy Policy:** [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- **TestFlight (Beta Testing):** Beta testers' usage data may be shared with Apple for crash reporting

## Data Retention

- **Local Data:** Your inventory data remains on your device until you delete items or uninstall the app
- **Backups:** Data may be included in device backups (iTunes, iCloud) according to your device settings
- **Deletion:** You can delete individual items or all data at any time through the app

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Access and Control

1. **Access Your Data**
   - All your data is accessible within the app
   - You can view, edit, or export any item at any time

2. **Delete Your Data**
   - Delete individual items through the app interface
   - Uninstall the app to remove all data from your device

3. **Photo Permissions**
   - You control whether the app can access your photo library
   - Revoke access anytime in Settings → Privacy → Photos

4. **Camera Permissions**
   - You control whether the app can access your camera for barcode scanning
   - Revoke access anytime in Settings → Privacy → Camera

### Opt-Out Options

- **Usage Analytics:** You can opt out of anonymous usage analytics in app Settings (when implemented)
- **API Lookups:** You can add items manually without using barcode scanning
- **iCloud Backup:** You can disable iCloud backup for the app in device Settings

## Children's Privacy

Physical-Digital Twins does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information as quickly as possible.

## Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement the following measures:

### On-Device Security

1. **Encryption**
   - All data is stored using iOS/visionOS standard encryption
   - Data is protected by your device passcode and biometric authentication

2. **Sandboxing**
   - The app runs in a sandboxed environment
   - Other apps cannot access your Physical-Digital Twins data

3. **Secure Connections**
   - All API calls use HTTPS/TLS encryption
   - Network communications are encrypted in transit

### Best Practices

- Keep your device updated with the latest visionOS version
- Use a strong device passcode or biometric authentication
- Enable "Find My" to remotely wipe your device if lost
