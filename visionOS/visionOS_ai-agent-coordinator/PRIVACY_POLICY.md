# Privacy Policy - AI Agent Coordinator

**Last Updated**: January 20, 2025

## Introduction

AI Agent Coordinator ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our visionOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: User ID for authentication purposes
- **Platform Credentials**: API keys for third-party AI platforms (stored encrypted locally)
- **Agent Configurations**: Settings and preferences for your AI agents
- **Workspace Data**: Your saved layouts, filters, and organizational preferences

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device ID, visionOS version, app version
- **Usage Data**: Features used, session duration (anonymous)
- **Performance Data**: App performance metrics, crash reports (anonymous)
- **Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app

### Information NOT Collected

We explicitly DO NOT collect:
- Personal identifiable information (name, email, address)
- Agent data content or payloads
- Your actual API requests or responses
- Location data
- Behavioral data for advertising

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide Services**: Enable app functionality
2. **Improve Quality**: Fix bugs, enhance features
3. **Ensure Security**: Detect and prevent fraud
4. **Support**: Respond to your inquiries
5. **Analytics**: Understand usage patterns (anonymous)

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- All sensitive data stored locally on your Vision Pro device
- Encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- Protected by visionOS security features

### iCloud Sync (Optional)

- If enabled, workspace data synced via iCloud
- End-to-end encrypted
- You control what syncs

### No Cloud Storage

We do NOT store your data on our servers.

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

- **Service Providers**: Only as necessary to provide services (e.g., crash reporting)
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law
- **Business Transfer**: In case of merger or acquisition (with notice)

## Third-Party Services

### Platform Integrations

When you connect to third-party platforms (OpenAI, AWS, etc.):
- Your credentials stored locally only
- Direct communication between your device and platform
- We act as a client, not an intermediary
- Subject to each platform's privacy policy

### Analytics Services

We may use:
- Apple Analytics (can be disabled in iOS Settings)
- Crash reporting (anonymous)

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: Request what data we have about you
2. **Delete**: Delete all your data from the app
3. **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics and crash reporting
4. **Export**: Export your agent configurations
5. **Correction**: Correct inaccurate information

### Exercising Your Rights

To exercise these rights:
- Settings > Privacy > Manage Data
- Email: privacy@aiagentcoordinator.dev

## Children's Privacy

AI Agent Coordinator is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Until you delete your account
- **Usage Analytics**: Retained for 90 days, then aggregated/anonymized
- **Crash Reports**: Retained for 1 year
- **Deleted Data**: Permanently deleted within 30 days

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
- Right to deletion
- Right to non-discrimination

We do NOT sell personal information.

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EEA residents have rights under GDPR:
- Right to access
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object

Contact our Data Protection Officer: dpo@aiagentcoordinator.dev

## Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track signals. When enabled, we disable analytics.
