Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with AI Agent Coordinator?

After installing the app on your Apple Vision Pro, launch it and complete the quick onboarding tutorial. You'll learn how to create your first AI agent, assign tasks, and visualize workflows in spatial dimensions.

What types of AI agents can I coordinate?

AI Agent Coordinator supports various agent types including task automation agents, data processing agents, monitoring agents, and custom-built agents. You can integrate with popular AI platforms and create custom agent configurations.

Can I collaborate with team members?

Yes! AI Agent Coordinator supports multi-user collaboration. Team members can view shared agent workflows, assign tasks, and monitor performance metrics together in real-time.

How many agents can I run simultaneously?

The number of concurrent agents depends on your subscription plan. The Basic plan supports up to 10 agents, Professional supports 50 agents, and Enterprise supports unlimited agents.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all agent communications and workflow data. Your configurations are stored securely in the cloud with enterprise-grade security measures. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

What visionOS features does the app utilize?

AI Agent Coordinator leverages spatial computing, hand tracking, eye tracking, and voice commands. You can arrange agent nodes in 3D space, use gestures to connect workflows, and control everything with natural interactions.

Can I export my agent configurations?

Yes, you can export agent configurations and workflows in JSON or YAML format. This allows you to backup your setups, share with team members, or migrate between environments.

Does the app work offline?

While internet connection is required for agent execution and cloud synchronization, you can view cached workflows and configurations offline. Any changes will sync when you reconnect.

How do I update the app?

AI Agent Coordinator updates automatically through the App Store. You can also manually check for updates in the App Store app on your Apple Vision Pro.

What are the system requirements?

AI Agent Coordinator requires Apple Vision Pro running visionOS 1.0 or later. We recommend at least 2GB of free storage space for optimal performance.
