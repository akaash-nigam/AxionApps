## 1. Spatial Computing Value

### Why does this need to be a spatial app vs traditional 2D?
Traditional e-learning forces abstract concepts through videos and slides, achieving only 10% retention after one week. Critical skills like equipment operation, customer interaction, and emergency procedures cannot be effectively learned by watching - they require practice. In spatial computing, learners physically perform tasks, building muscle memory and spatial understanding. This experiential learning achieves 90% retention, 4x faster skill acquisition, and dramatically higher confidence when performing real-world tasks. The difference between watching a safety video and practicing emergency procedures in VR can literally save lives.
