# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Corporate University Platform

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

The Corporate University Platform transforms employee learning from passive video courses into immersive spatial experiences where learners walk through knowledge landscapes, practice skills in realistic simulations, collaborate with global colleagues in virtual classrooms, and leverage AI tutors that adapt to individual learning styles. This spatial platform enables organizations to upskill workforces 10x faster, retain knowledge 5x longer, and create continuous learning cultures that drive competitive advantage in the AI era.
