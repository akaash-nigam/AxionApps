# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Spatial HCM (Human Capital Management)

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

Spatial HCM revolutionizes human capital management by transforming employee data into living organizational ecosystems where HR leaders, managers, and employees navigate talent landscapes, cultivate performance gardens, and orchestrate workforce dynamics through immersive 3D experiences. This platform enables organizations to literally see their human capital as interconnected networks, identify talent gaps as spatial voids, and use AI-powered insights to predict and shape workforce futures with unprecedented precision.

### Strategic Vision
* **Business Transformation**: From HR administration to spatial talent orchestration
* **Competitive Advantage**: 360° view of organizational health and potential
* **AI Integration**: Predictive workforce analytics and autonomous talent matching
* **Platform Strategy**: Become the living nervous system of organizational culture
