Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

After installing Executive Briefing on your Apple Vision Pro, launch the app and follow the quick onboarding tutorial to learn the basic features and controls.

What are the system requirements?

Executive Briefing requires Apple Vision Pro running visionOS 1.0 or later. We recommend at least 2GB of free storage space for optimal performance.

Can I use this app offline?

Some features require an internet connection for full functionality. However, core features remain available offline with data syncing when you reconnect.

How do I report a bug?

You can report bugs directly through the app's settings menu or by contacting our support team. Please include details about what you were doing when the issue occurred.

Is there a tutorial?

Yes! Access the interactive tutorial anytime from the main menu. It covers all major features and controls with hands-on practice.

How do I update the app?

Executive Briefing updates automatically through the App Store. You can also manually check for updates in the App Store app on your Apple Vision Pro.

Still Need Help?

Our support team is here to assist you with any questions or issues.
