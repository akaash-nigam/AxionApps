# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Industrial CAD/CAM Suite

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

The Industrial CAD/CAM Suite transforms manufacturing design and production from 2D screens and physical prototypes into immersive spatial environments where engineers sculpt products with their hands, walk through assemblies at any scale, simulate manufacturing processes in real-time, and leverage AI to optimize designs for cost, performance, and manufacturability. This spatial platform enables teams to reduce development cycles by 70%, eliminate costly prototypes, and manufacture products right the first time through perfect digital-physical synchronization.

### Strategic Vision
* **Business Transformation**: From sequential design-build-test to simultaneous spatial engineering
* **Competitive Advantage**: 10x faster product development with zero-defect manufacturing
* **AI Integration**: Generative design with real-time optimization and validation
* **Platform Strategy**: Become the spatial OS for global manufacturing innovation
