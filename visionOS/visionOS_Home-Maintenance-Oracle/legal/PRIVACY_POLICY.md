# Privacy Policy
## Home Maintenance Oracle

**Effective Date**: January 1, 2025
**Last Updated**: November 24, 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to Home Maintenance Oracle ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our visionOS application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide to Us

**Appliance Information**
- Brand, model, and serial numbers of appliances you add
- Purchase dates and warranty information
- Installation dates and locations (room/area)
- Photos of appliances (stored locally on your device)
- Custom notes and descriptions

**Maintenance Records**
- Maintenance tasks and schedules
- Task completion dates and notes
- Service history
- Maintenance photos and documentation

**User Preferences**
- Notification preferences
- App settings and customizations
- Theme preferences

### 1.2 Information Automatically Collected

**Device and Usage Information**
- Device type (Apple Vision Pro)
- Operating system version (visionOS)
- App version
- Device identifiers (for crash reporting)
- Usage statistics (features used, frequency)
- Performance metrics (app crashes, errors)

**Camera Access** (Optional)
- When you use the appliance scanning feature, the App accesses your device camera to capture images
- **Images are processed entirely on your device** using on-device machine learning
- Images used for recognition are **not stored** and are discarded after processing
- Only images you explicitly save to your inventory are retained
- Camera access can be revoked at any time through Settings

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Payment information (the App does not currently process payments)
- Location data beyond what you manually enter
- Biometric data
- Contact lists or social graphs
- Information from other apps
- Web browsing history

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

### 2.1 Provide and Maintain the App
- Store your appliance inventory locally on your device
- Generate maintenance schedules based on manufacturer recommendations
- Send you notifications for upcoming maintenance tasks
- Display your service history and records

### 2.2 Improve Our Services
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features (anonymized)
- Debug and fix technical issues
- Optimize app performance
- Develop new features based on usage insights

### 2.3 Machine Learning
- Process appliance images on-device to identify brands and models
- Improve recognition accuracy (model updates do not use your data)
- All ML processing happens **locally on your device**
- No images or recognition data are sent to our servers

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage

**All your data is stored locally on your Apple Vision Pro device**:
- Appliance information
- Maintenance records
- Photos and documents
- App settings

Data is stored using Apple's Core Data framework with standard security measures.

### 4.2 iCloud Backup (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync:
- Your data is backed up to your personal iCloud account
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Only you can access your iCloud data
- You can disable iCloud sync at any time

### 4.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Data encryption on device
- Secure data transmission (if applicable)
- Regular security audits
- No remote data access

However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

**Access Your Data**
- View all data stored in the App through the app interface
- Export your data (if feature is available)

**Modify Your Data**
- Edit appliance information
- Update maintenance records
- Change app settings

**Delete Your Data**
- Delete individual appliances and records
- Delete all app data through Settings
- Uninstall the App to remove all data

### 5.2 Camera Permissions

You can control camera access:
- Grant or revoke camera access in Settings > Privacy > Camera
- Camera is only used when you initiate scanning
- You can add appliances manually without camera access

### 5.3 Notification Permissions

You can control notifications:
- Grant or revoke notification access in Settings > Notifications
- Customize which notifications you receive in the App
- Disable all notifications without affecting core functionality

### 5.4 Regional Privacy Rights

**For European Union Users (GDPR)**
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

**For California Users (CCPA)**
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Right to deletion of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at: [support@homemaintenanceoracle.com]
