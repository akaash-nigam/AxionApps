## Risks & Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Risk**: Recognition accuracy varies
  - **Mitigation**: Continuous model training, manual fallback
- **Risk**: Performance issues at scale
  - **Mitigation**: Early optimization, load testing
- **Risk**: visionOS API changes
  - **Mitigation**: Track beta releases, stay updated

### Market Risks
- **Risk**: Low visionOS adoption
  - **Mitigation**: Expand to iOS/Android early
- **Risk**: Competition emerges
  - **Mitigation**: Focus on quality, community, differentiation
- **Risk**: Limited monetization
  - **Mitigation**: Test pricing, find PMF, add value

### Operational Risks
- **Risk**: Support volume too high
  - **Mitigation**: Great docs, in-app help, community
- **Risk**: Legal/compliance issues
  - **Mitigation**: Legal review, privacy-first, compliance
