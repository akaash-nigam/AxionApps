# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Financial Operations Platform

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

The Financial Operations Platform transforms financial management from spreadsheets and dashboards into immersive financial universes where CFOs navigate cash flow rivers, treasurers optimize liquidity landscapes in real-time, controllers walk through close processes, and AI predicts financial risks before they materialize. This spatial platform enables finance teams to see money flow through the organization, identify optimization opportunities instantly, and make decisions that improve working capital, reduce costs, and accelerate growth.

* **Competitive Advantage**: 50% faster close, 30% better cash optimization
