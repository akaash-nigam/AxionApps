## Introduction

Welcome to Wardrobe Consultant ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

**Privacy-First Commitment:** Wardrobe Consultant is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your personal data stays on your device, and we minimize data collection to only what is necessary for the app to function.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

#### Account Information
- **Not Collected**: We do not require account creation or email addresses for basic app functionality
- **Optional**: If you choose to create an account for cloud sync (future feature), we will collect your email address

#### Wardrobe Data
- **Photos**: Images of your clothing items stored locally on your device
- **Item Details**: Category, brand, color, size, price, purchase date, and other metadata you enter
- **Measurements**: Body measurements (stored securely in Keychain, never transmitted)
- **Preferences**: Style preferences, favorite colors, preferred sizes

#### Usage Data
- **Outfit History**: Record of outfits you've worn (stored locally)
- **Favorites**: Items and outfits you've marked as favorites
- **App Interactions**: Features you use and settings you configure

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

#### Device Information
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Device identifiers (for crash reporting only)

#### Usage Analytics (Optional)
- App launch events
- Feature usage statistics
- Performance metrics
- Crash reports

**Note**: Analytics are collected anonymously and can be disabled in Settings.

### 3. Information from Third-Party Services

#### Calendar Integration (Optional)
- Event names, dates, and times
- **Purpose**: Provide outfit recommendations for upcoming events
- **Storage**: Processed locally, not stored permanently

#### Weather Service (Optional)
- Current location (approximate)
- **Purpose**: Provide weather-based outfit recommendations
- **Storage**: Location not stored; only current weather data cached temporarily

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
**Primary Storage Location**: All your personal data is stored locally on your device using:
- **Core Data**: Encrypted SQLite database for wardrobe data
- **Keychain Services**: Secure storage for sensitive information (measurements)
- **File System**: Encrypted photo storage with NSFileProtectionComplete

### Security Measures
- **Encryption at Rest**: All data encrypted on device
- **Secure Storage**: Sensitive data stored in iOS Keychain
- **No Cloud Storage**: Personal data not transmitted to our servers (unless cloud sync feature is enabled)
- **Photo Protection**: Photos stored with file-level encryption

### Access Control
- **Biometric Authentication**: Optional Face ID/Touch ID for app access
- **Device Lock**: App data protected by device passcode
- **Automatic Lock**: App can be configured to require authentication after timeout

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control
You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored in the app
- **Correct**: Edit or update your information
- **Delete**: Remove individual items or all data
- **Export**: Export your wardrobe data (future feature)
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics and optional features

### Data Deletion
To delete your data:
1. Open Settings
2. Tap "Delete All Data"
3. Confirm deletion

**Note**: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

### California Privacy Rights (CCPA)
If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect
- Request deletion of your information
- Opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

### European Privacy Rights (GDPR)
If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Object to processing
- Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@wardrobeconsultant.app
