## Dependencies & Risks

### Technical Dependencies
- Apple platform stability
- ML model performance
- CloudKit reliability
- Partner API availability

### Business Dependencies
- App Store approval
- User acquisition costs
- Brand partnerships
- Funding availability

### Risks & Mitigation

**Risk:** Vision Pro adoption slower than expected
**Mitigation:** Strong iOS experience, platform flexibility

**Risk:** Privacy regulations change
**Mitigation:** Local-first architecture, compliance monitoring

**Risk:** Competitor launches similar product
**Mitigation:** Fast execution, unique visionOS features, brand loyalty

**Risk:** User acquisition costs too high
**Mitigation:** Organic growth, community features, viral sharing
