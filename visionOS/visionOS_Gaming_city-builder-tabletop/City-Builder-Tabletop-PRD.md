# City Builder Tabletop - Product Requirements Document
*Spatial Gaming in the AI Era*

---

## Section 1: Game Concept & Abstract

City Builder Tabletop revolutionizes urban planning simulation by allowing players to construct and manage entire cities on any flat surface using Vision Pro's spatial capabilities. Whether on your dining table, coffee table, or floor, watch as detailed 3D cities emerge and evolve, complete with traffic flow, citizen AI, and dynamic economic systems that respond to your architectural decisions in real-time. This transforms both gaming and professional urban planning by making complex simulations accessible through natural spatial interaction.

### Core Elements
* **Genre & Setting**: Urban simulation and city-building with real-world surface integration
* **Unique Spatial Hook**: Any flat surface becomes a living, breathing metropolis with persistent growth
* **AI Integration**: Citizen behavior simulation, traffic optimization, economic modeling, collaborative decision-making
* **Target Platform**: Utilizes Vision Pro's surface detection, hand tracking, multi-user collaboration, and spatial persistence
