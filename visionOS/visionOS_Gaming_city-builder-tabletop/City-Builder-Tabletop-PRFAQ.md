**Spatial Experience**

Using natural gestures, players zone areas, draw roads that automatically generate realistic intersections, and place buildings that construct themselves with satisfying animations. Lean close to hear city sounds and observe citizen moods. Problems manifest visually - traffic jams form, protests gather at city hall, and emergency vehicles rush to incidents. Night brings twinkling lights and reduced activity cycles.
