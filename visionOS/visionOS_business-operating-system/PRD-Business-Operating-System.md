## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

The Business Operating System (BOS) represents a revolutionary unified spatial computing platform that transforms how enterprises operate by consolidating ERP, CRM, HCM, BI, and collaboration tools into a single immersive 3D environment. Built for Apple Vision Pro, BOS enables executives and employees to visualize, interact with, and optimize their entire business through spatial interfaces, while AI agents autonomously handle routine operations and provide predictive insights.
