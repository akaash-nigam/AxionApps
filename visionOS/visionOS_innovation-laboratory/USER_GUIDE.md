## Getting Started

### System Requirements

- **Device:** Apple Vision Pro
- **Operating System:** visionOS 2.0 or later
- **Storage:** Minimum 500MB available space
- **Memory:** Recommended 2GB free RAM
- **Network:** Wi-Fi connection (for collaboration features)

### Installation

1. **Download from App Store**
   - Open the App Store on your Vision Pro
   - Search for "Innovation Laboratory"
   - Tap "Get" or the price button
   - Authenticate with Face ID, Optic ID, or password
   - Wait for download and installation

2. **Initial Setup**
   - Launch Innovation Laboratory from Home View
   - Grant necessary permissions when prompted
   - Sign in with your organizational account
   - Complete the onboarding tutorial (optional but recommended)

## Capturing Ideas

### Starting a New Idea

**Method 1: Quick Capture (Fastest)**
1. From anywhere, look at "New Idea" button
2. Tap with your hand or pinch gesture
3. Speak or type your idea
4. Tap "Save"

**Method 2: Detailed Form**
1. Tap "New Idea" button
2. Fill in the detailed form:
   - **Title** (required) - Short, descriptive name
   - **Description** (required) - Detailed explanation
   - **Category** - Select from predefined categories
   - **Priority** - Low, Medium, High, Critical
   - **Tags** - Add searchable keywords
   - **Attachments** - Upload supporting files

**Method 3: Voice Capture (Hands-Free)**
1. Say "Hey Siri, capture innovation idea"
2. Speak your idea naturally
3. Review and confirm

### Idea Categories

Choose the category that best fits your idea:

- üíº **Product** - New products or product improvements
- üîß **Technology** - Technical innovations
- üìä **Process** - Workflow improvements
- üéØ **Business Model** - Revenue or business strategy
- üé® **Design** - UI/UX or aesthetic improvements
- üë• **Customer Experience** - Customer-facing enhancements
- ‚ôªÔ∏è **Sustainability** - Environmental initiatives
- üåç **Other** - Doesn't fit other categories

### Adding Attachments

Support for multiple file types:
- **Images** - PNG, JPG, HEIC (up to 10MB)
- **Documents** - PDF, DOC, TXT (up to 20MB)
- **Videos** - MP4, MOV (up to 100MB)
- **3D Models** - USDZ, OBJ, FBX (for prototype visualization)

**To Attach Files:**
1. Tap "Add Attachment" in idea form
2. Select source (Photos, Files, Camera)
3. Choose file(s)
4. Add optional description for each file

## Managing Ideas

### Viewing Your Ideas

**List View (Default)**
- Scroll through all ideas

## Creating Prototypes

Transform ideas into testable prototypes.

### Starting a New Prototype

1. Open an idea
2. Tap "Create Prototype" button
3. Fill in prototype details:
   - **Name** - Descriptive prototype name
   - **Version** - Semantic versioning (e.g., 1.0.0)
   - **Type** - Physical, Digital, Service, Hybrid
   - **Description** - What this prototype tests

### Prototype Types

- **üñ•Ô∏è Digital** - Software, apps, websites
- **üîß Physical** - Hardware, products, devices
- **ü§ù Service** - Customer service, support processes

### Adding 3D Models

1. Tap "Add 3D Model" in prototype

- FBX

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### App Won't Launch

**Symptoms:** App crashes immediately or shows blank screen

**Solutions:**
1. Force quit: Double-press Digital Crown > Swipe up on app
2. Restart Vision Pro: Hold Digital Crown + Top button
3. Update visionOS: Settings > General > Software Update
4. Reinstall app: Delete > Download from App Store

#### Ideas Not Syncing

**Symptoms:** Changes don't appear on other devices

**Solutions:**
1. Check Wi-Fi connection
2. Verify iCloud is enabled
3. Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Innovation Laboratory: ON
4. Force sync: Settings > Advanced > Sync Now

#### Collaboration Session Fails

**Symptoms:** Can't join or start collaboration

**Solutions:**
1. Verify all users on same Wi-Fi
2. Check FaceTime is enabled
3. Ensure both users signed into iCloud
4. Try direct invite instead of FaceTime
5. Restart both devices

#### Prototype Studio Laggy

**Symptoms:** Low frame rate, stuttering in Studio

**Solutions:**
1. Close other apps
2. Settings > Performance > Quality: Medium
3. Reduce prototype complexity
4. Restart app
5. Free up storage space

#### 3D Model Won't Load

**Symptoms:** "Error loading model" message

**Solutions:**
1. Verify file format is USDZ, OBJ, or FBX
2. Check file size <100MB
3. Try re-exporting from 3D software
4. Use Reality Converter to convert to USDZ
5. Check for corrupted file

### Performance Issues

**App Running Slow:**
1. Close unused windows/volumes
2. Clear cache: Settings > Data & Storage > Clear Cache
3. Archive old ideas
4. Restart app
5. Restart Vision Pro

**Battery Draining Fast:**
1. Exit immersive mode when not needed
2. Reduce frame rate target
3. Disable background sync
4. Lower quality settings
5. Close app when not in use

### Error Messages

**"Storage Full"**
- Delete unused attachments
- Archive or delete old ideas
- Clear cache
- Offload other apps

**"Cannot Connect"**
- Check Wi-Fi connection
- Verify internet access
- Check app permissions
- Try airplane mode toggle

**"Permission Denied"**
- Settings > Privacy > Grant necessary permissions
- May need camera, microphone, local network
