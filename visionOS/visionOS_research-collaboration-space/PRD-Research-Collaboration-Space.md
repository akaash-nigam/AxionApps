# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Research Collaboration Space

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

The Research Collaboration Space transforms scientific collaboration from fragmented video calls and email chains into immersive research universes where global teams work side-by-side in virtual laboratories, manipulate data together in real-time, share insights through spatial knowledge graphs, and leverage AI to accelerate discoveries across disciplines. This spatial platform enables research teams to collaborate 10x more effectively, reduce time to breakthrough by 70%, and solve humanity's greatest challenges through unprecedented scientific cooperation.
