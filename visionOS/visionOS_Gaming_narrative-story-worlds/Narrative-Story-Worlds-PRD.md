## Section 1: Game Concept & Abstract

Narrative Story Worlds transforms players' living spaces into immersive, emotionally-driven story experiences where choices reshape the environment, characters remember and react to past decisions, and AI weaves personalized narratives that adapt to individual play styles. Players become protagonists in cinematic adventures that unfold around them, featuring photorealistic characters who engage in meaningful conversations, branching storylines that lead to dramatically different outcomes, and spatial puzzles that utilize their actual room layout. This revolutionary storytelling platform proves that spatial computing can deliver emotional narratives rivaling the best films and novels.

### Core Elements
* **Genre & Setting**: Narrative adventure across multiple story worlds (mystery, drama, sci-fi, fantasy)
* **Unique Spatial Hook**: Your room becomes the story stage with characters and objects appearing naturally
* **AI Integration**: Dynamic story adaptation, character personalities that evolve, dialogue generation
* **Target Platform**: Vision Pro's eye tracking for emotional reads, hand gestures for choices
