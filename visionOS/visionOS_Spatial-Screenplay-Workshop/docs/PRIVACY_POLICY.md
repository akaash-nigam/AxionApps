# Privacy Policy for Spatial Screenplay Workshop

**Last Updated**: November 24, 2025
**Effective Date**: November 24, 2025

## Introduction

Spatial Screenplay Workshop ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our visionOS application.

## TL;DR (Summary)

- ✅ We **DON'T** collect any personal information
- ✅ We **DON'T** track you or use analytics
- ✅ All your screenplays stay **on your device**
- ✅ No account required, no login, no sign-up
- ✅ Future iCloud sync will be **optional** and **opt-in**

## Information We DO NOT Collect

We designed Spatial Screenplay Workshop with privacy as a core principle. We **DO NOT** collect, store, or transmit:

- ❌ Personal identification information
- ❌ Email addresses or contact information
- ❌ Usage analytics or behavior tracking
- ❌ Advertising identifiers
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ IP addresses
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Crash reports (unless you opt-in)
- ❌ Any other personal data

## Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored **only on your Apple Vision Pro** using Apple's secure SwiftData framework:

### Screenplay Projects
- Project titles and metadata
- Scene content and structure
- Character information
- Notes and annotations
- User preferences and settings

**Storage Location**: Your device's local storage
**Encryption**: Protected by visionOS security
**Backup**: Included in device backups (encrypted)
**Access**: Only you, via the App

### Cache and Temporary Files
- Auto-save snapshots (5-minute intervals)
- Undo/redo history
- PDF export temporary files

**Retention**: Automatically cleaned up
**Access**: Only the App

## Optional Features (Opt-In Only)

### iCloud Sync (Future - v1.1)

**What it is**: Optionally sync your projects across your Apple devices

**How it works**:
- Disabled by default
- You must explicitly enable in Settings
- Uses Apple's CloudKit (covered by Apple's privacy policy)
- Data encrypted in transit and at rest
- You can disable anytime

**What we see**: Nothing. Your data goes directly to your iCloud account.

### Crash Reporting (Future)

**What it is**: Help us fix bugs by sending anonymous crash reports

**How it works**:
- Opt-in only in Settings
- Uses Apple's crash reporting
- No personal information included
- Only technical diagnostic data

### Anonymous Usage Analytics (Future)

**What it is**: Help us improve by sharing anonymous usage patterns

**How it works**:
- Opt-in only in Settings
- No personally identifiable information
- Aggregated statistics only
- Examples: "Feature X used by Y% of users"

**What we track** (if you opt-in):
- App launches
- Feature usage counts
- Performance metrics (FPS, load times)
- Export format preferences

**What we DON'T track**:
- Screenplay content
- Project names
- Personal information
- Individual user behavior

## Third-Party Services

We use **ZERO** third-party services. Your data never leaves your device.

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT** sell, trade, rent, or share your data with anyone. Period.

**Exception**: We must comply with valid legal requests (subpoenas, court orders). We will:
- Notify you if legally permitted
- Only provide minimum required information
- Challenge overly broad requests

**In our entire history**: Zero legal requests received (as of this date).

## Children's Privacy

Spatial Screenplay Workshop is rated 4+ and safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or adults. If you're a parent concerned about privacy, rest assured: the App doesn't collect anything.

## Data Retention

**Local Data**: Kept until you delete projects or uninstall the App

**iCloud Data** (future): Kept until you:
- Delete projects manually
- Disable iCloud sync
- Delete your iCloud account

**Analytics** (future, opt-in): Aggregated data kept for 2 years, then deleted

## Your Privacy Rights

### You Have the Right To:

✅ **Access**: View all your local data in the App
✅ **Delete**: Delete any project or all data
✅ **Export**: Export to PDF or Fountain format
✅ **Opt-out**: Disable all optional features
✅ **Request information**: Email us about our practices

### How to Exercise Your Rights:

**Delete All Data**:
1. Open Settings in the App
2. Tap "Delete All Projects"
3. Confirm deletion

**Delete Single Project**:
1. Select project
2. Tap delete button
3. Confirm deletion

**Disable iCloud Sync** (future):
1. Open Settings
2. Toggle "iCloud Sync" off
3. Choose to keep or delete cloud data

## Data Security

We protect your data through:

- **Device Security**: visionOS sandboxing and file encryption
- **No Network Transmission**: Data stays on your device (until you opt-in to sync)
- **Secure Coding Practices**: Regular security audits
- **No Third Parties**: Zero external dependencies in v1.0

## International Users
