**Spatial Experience**
Performances begin as holographic actors enter your space, acknowledging the room and audience. Unlike traditional theater, characters make eye contact, ask questions, and respond to gestures. Key decision points pause action for audience input - save a character from danger, reveal hidden information, or stay silent and watch consequences unfold. Advanced AI ensures responses feel natural while maintaining narrative coherence. Multiple viewing reveals different story branches, with some productions featuring over 100 possible variations. Social viewing allows friends to collectively influence outcomes, creating unique shared experiences.
