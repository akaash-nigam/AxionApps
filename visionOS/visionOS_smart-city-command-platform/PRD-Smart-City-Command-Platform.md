# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Smart City Command Platform

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

The Smart City Command Platform transforms urban management into an immersive 3D control center where city officials navigate through living digital twins of their metropolis, orchestrate services across departments, and leverage AI to predict and prevent urban challenges before they impact citizens. This spatial platform enables city leaders to literally walk through neighborhoods, visualize infrastructure flows, and coordinate emergency responses through natural gestures while ensuring citizen privacy and democratic governance.

### Strategic Vision
* **Business Transformation**: From reactive city management to predictive urban orchestration
* **Competitive Advantage**: 360° city visibility enabling proactive governance
* **AI Integration**: Autonomous optimization with citizen-centric oversight
* **Platform Strategy**: Become the operating system for smart cities worldwide
