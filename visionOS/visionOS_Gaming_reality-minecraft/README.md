# Reality Minecraft
**Tagline:** "Build, explore, and survive in your actual world"

## Overview
Reality Minecraft revolutionizes the beloved block-building experience by overlaying the Minecraft universe directly onto your real-world environment. Using Vision Pro's spatial mapping, players mine virtual blocks from real walls, place constructions that persist in specific physical locations, and encounter Minecraft creatures that navigate your actual furniture and room layout, creating an unprecedented fusion of digital creativity and physical space.

## Core Gameplay Features

### Spatial Block Building
- **Real-World Integration**: Place blocks on actual surfaces with perfect alignment and persistence
- **Mixed Material Mining**: Extract different block types from walls, floors, and furniture
- **Gravity-Defying Construction**: Build floating structures anchored to your physical environment
- **Multi-Room Expansion**: Extend builds across entire homes with room-to-room continuity

### Augmented Survival Mode
- **Environmental Spawning**: Monsters emerge from dark corners and hide behind real furniture
- **Resource Gathering**: Mine materials from your actual environment with spatial accuracy
- **Shelter Building**: Construct protective structures that interact with your room's layout
- **Day/Night Cycles**: Lighting changes affect both virtual elements and real room ambiance

### Creative Freedom Enhanced
- **Unlimited Scale**: Build structures limited only by your available physical space
- **Collaborative Building**: Multiple players construct together in the same physical location
- **Real-Physics Integration**: Virtual blocks interact with gravity and real-world surfaces
- **Export Capabilities**: Share builds with traditional Minecraft and other platforms

## Technical Innovation

### Advanced Spatial Persistence
- **Location-Locked Builds**: Constructions remain exactly where placed between sessions
- **Multi-Surface Anchoring**: Blocks attach to walls, ceilings, and complex furniture geometries
- **Real-World Physics**: Virtual constructions obey gravity while maintaining magical building possibilities
- **Cross-Device Compatibility**: Builds synchronize across multiple Vision Pro devices

### Intelligent Environmental Adaptation
- **Furniture Recognition**: Blocks automatically adjust to sit properly on tables, shelves, and surfaces
- **Lighting Integration**: Minecraft's lighting system responds to your actual room lighting
- **Obstacle Navigation**: Minecraft mobs pathfind around real furniture and room layouts
- **Safety Boundaries**: Virtual elements respect physical movement spaces and safety requirements
