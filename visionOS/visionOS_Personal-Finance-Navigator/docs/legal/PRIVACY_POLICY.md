# Privacy Policy
# Personal Finance Navigator

**Effective Date**: November 24, 2025

## Introduction

Personal Finance Navigator ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application for Apple Vision Pro.

**By using Personal Finance Navigator, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

#### Account Information
- Account names and types (checking, savings, credit card, etc.)
- Account balances and transaction history
- Budget information and financial goals
- Custom categories and notes

#### Personal Preferences
- Currency preferences
- Budget strategies
- Notification settings
- Security preferences (biometric auth, auto-lock timeout)

### Information Automatically Collected

#### Device Information
- Device type (Apple Vision Pro)
- Operating system version (visionOS)
- App version
- Device identifiers (for iCloud sync only)

#### Usage Information
- Features used within the app
- Crash reports and error logs
- Performance metrics

#### Biometric Data
- Face ID / Optic ID authentication data (stored locally on device only)
- **Never leaves your device**
- **Never transmitted to our servers**

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses

- **Financial Management**: Process and display your financial data
- **Data Synchronization**: Sync your data across your Apple devices via iCloud
- **Security**: Authenticate your identity and protect your data
- **Improvement**: Improve app performance and user experience
- **Support**: Provide customer support and troubleshoot issues

### We Do NOT Use Your Data For

- ❌ **Advertising**: We never sell or share your data for advertising
- ❌ **Third-Party Analytics**: No third-party analytics or tracking
- ❌ **Profiling**: No user profiling or behavior tracking
- ❌ **Marketing**: No unsolicited marketing communications

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- **Core Data**: Financial data stored locally on your device
- **Encrypted**: AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive data
- **Keychain**: Secure storage for authentication tokens and encryption keys

### Cloud Storage

- **iCloud Private CloudKit**: Optional sync across your devices
- **End-to-End Encryption**: Data encrypted before leaving your device
- **Apple's Security**: Leverages Apple's secure infrastructure
- **Your Control**: You can disable iCloud sync at any time

### Security Measures

- **Biometric Authentication**: Face ID / Optic ID protection
- **Auto-Lock**: Automatic app locking after inactivity
- **App Backgrounding**: Screen obscured when app is in background
- **Secure Communication**: HTTPS for all network requests
- **No Plain Text**: Sensitive data never stored in plain text

## Third-Party Services

### Plaid (Banking Connection)

**When You Choose to Connect Your Bank:**
- Plaid provides secure bank connection services
- Your bank credentials are **never** stored by us
- Plaid uses bank-level encryption (256-bit)
- You can revoke access at any time
- Read Plaid's Privacy Policy: [https://plaid.com/legal/](https://plaid.com/legal/)

**What Plaid Collects:**
- Bank account information (account numbers, balances)
- Transaction history
- Account holder information

**How We Use Plaid Data:**
- Import transactions automatically
- Update account balances
- Categorize transactions

### Apple Services

- **iCloud**: For data synchronization (optional)
- **App Store**: For app distribution and updates
- **Apple ID**: For authentication and device management

**Apple's Privacy Policy**: [https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View all your data within the app
- **Edit**: Modify or update your information
- **Delete**: Remove data from the app
- **Export**: Download your data (coming soon)
- **Opt-Out**: Disable iCloud sync or bank connections

### How to Exercise Your Rights

**Within the App:**
- Settings → Privacy → Manage Your Data
- Settings → Account → Export Data
- Settings → Account → Delete All Data

**Contact Us:**
- Email: privacy@pfnavigator.app
- Response time: Within 30 days

## Data Retention

### How Long We Keep Your Data

- **Active Use**: Data retained while you use the app
- **After Deletion**: Permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Backups**: Removed from backups within 90 days
- **iCloud**: Controlled by your Apple ID settings

### How to Delete Your Data

1. Open Personal Finance Navigator
2. Go to Settings → Account
3. Tap "Delete All Data"
4. Confirm deletion
5. Data is permanently removed

## Children's Privacy

Personal Finance Navigator is **not intended for users under 18 years of age.**

- We do not knowingly collect information from children
- If we discover data from a child, we will delete it immediately
- Parents: Contact us if you believe your child provided data

### EU Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights:

- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Portability**: Receive your data in a structured format
- **Right to Object**: Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact: gdpr@pfnavigator.app

### California Users (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

- Know what personal information is collected
- Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Say no to the sale of personal information
- Access their personal information
- Request deletion of personal information

**We do not sell California residents' personal information.**
