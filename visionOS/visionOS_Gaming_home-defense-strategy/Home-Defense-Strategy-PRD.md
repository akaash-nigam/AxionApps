# Home Defense Strategy - Product Requirements Document
*Spatial Gaming in the AI Era*

---

## Section 1: Game Concept & Abstract

Home Defense Strategy transforms your actual living space into an immersive tower defense game where virtual enemies attempt to breach your real-world environment. Using Vision Pro's spatial mapping and tracking, players place defensive turrets, barriers, and traps throughout their home to defend against waves of AR enemies that seamlessly interact with your furniture, walls, and rooms. This revolutionary spatial strategy experience makes your familiar environment the ultimate customizable battlefield.

### Core Elements
* **Genre & Setting**: Spatial tower defense with real-world integration
* **Unique Spatial Hook**: Your actual home becomes the battlefield with persistent defensive installations
* **AI Integration**: Adaptive enemy pathfinding, smart turret automation, dynamic wave generation
* **Target Platform**: Utilizes Vision Pro's spatial mapping, hand tracking, and persistent anchoring
