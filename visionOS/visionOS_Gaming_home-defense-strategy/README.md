# Home Defense Strategy
**Tagline:** "Turn your living space into the ultimate strategic battlefield"

## Overview
Home Defense Strategy transforms your actual living space into an immersive tower defense game where virtual enemies attempt to breach your real-world environment. Using Vision Pro's spatial mapping and tracking, players place defensive turrets, barriers, and traps throughout their home to defend against waves of AR enemies that seamlessly interact with your furniture, walls, and rooms.

## Core Gameplay Features

### Spatial Tower Defense
- **Real-World Integration**: Enemies navigate around your actual furniture and use real doorways and corridors
- **Strategic Placement**: Position turrets on tables, walls, and shelves with perfect spatial accuracy
- **Multi-Room Campaigns**: Defend multiple rooms simultaneously with interconnected strategy layers
- **Environmental Tactics**: Use real furniture as cover, create chokepoints with virtual barriers

### Advanced Defense Systems
- **Smart Turret AI**: Automated defenses that learn your home's layout and optimize firing patterns
- **Trap Networks**: Deploy invisible tripwires and proximity mines that enemies must navigate
- **Resource Management**: Collect energy from defeated enemies to upgrade your defensive grid
- **Emergency Protocols**: Activate panic room mode for high-intensity final stand scenarios

### Dynamic Enemy AI
- **Spatial Pathfinding**: Enemies realistically navigate your home's unique layout
- **Adaptive Behavior**: AI learns from your defensive strategies and counters them
- **Environmental Interaction**: Enemies can hide behind couches, climb stairs, and use realistic physics
- **Escalating Threats**: Each wave introduces new enemy types with unique movement patterns

## Technical Innovation

### Spatial Mapping Excellence
- **Persistent World State**: Your defensive installations remain exactly where you place them between sessions
- **Real-Time Physics**: Virtual objects perfectly interact with real surfaces and gravity
- **Lighting Integration**: Game lighting responds to your actual room lighting conditions
- **Hand Tracking Precision**: Place and adjust defenses with millimeter accuracy using natural gestures

### AR Integration
- **Seamless Reality Blend**: Virtual enemies cast realistic shadows and reflect in mirrors
- **Occlusion Accuracy**: Objects correctly hide behind real-world furniture
- **Spatial Audio**: 3D positional audio lets you hear enemies approaching from any direction
- **Haptic Feedback**: Feel turret vibrations through hand tracking when weapons fire
