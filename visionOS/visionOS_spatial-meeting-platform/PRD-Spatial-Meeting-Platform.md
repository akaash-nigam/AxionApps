# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Spatial Meeting Platform

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

The Spatial Meeting Platform revolutionizes remote collaboration by transforming traditional video calls into immersive 3D environments where participants feel genuinely present together. This platform enables teams to meet in virtual boardrooms, brainstorm on infinite whiteboards, manipulate 3D data collaboratively, and build relationships through natural spatial interactions, all while AI facilitates seamless communication, captures decisions, and ensures productive outcomes.

### Strategic Vision
* **Business Transformation**: From exhausting video calls to energizing spatial collaboration
* **Competitive Advantage**: 10x more effective than traditional video conferencing
* **AI Integration**: Intelligent meeting facilitation and automatic documentation
* **Platform Strategy**: Become the default platform for enterprise spatial collaboration
