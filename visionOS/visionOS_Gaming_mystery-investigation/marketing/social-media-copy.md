## Platform Overview

### Social Media Strategy

**Overall Goal**: Build community of detective game enthusiasts and educate about spatial computing gaming

**Target Audience by Platform**:
- **Twitter/X**: Tech enthusiasts, gamers, mainstream audience
- **Instagram**: Younger demographic (18-35), visual storytellers
- **Facebook**: Older demographic (35+), families, community builders
- **TikTok**: Gen Z and younger millennials, short-form video
- **LinkedIn**: B2B partnerships, education institutions, corporate training
- **Reddit**: Gaming communities, specific interest groups

**Posting Frequency**:
- **Twitter/X**: 2-3 posts daily
- **Instagram**: 4-5 posts weekly
- **Facebook**: 2-3 posts daily
- **TikTok**: 5-7 videos weekly
- **LinkedIn**: 2-3 posts weekly
- **Reddit**: Participation in relevant communities (not direct promotion)
