Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

1. Introduction

Mystery Investigation ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Mystery Investigation application for Apple Vision Pro (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process personal information in connection with our App and website.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our App and website. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, password, date of birth, and profile preferences.
  • Payment Information: When you make a purchase, we collect payment information including credit card details (processed securely through our payment processor). We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Communication: When you contact us for support, feedback, or inquiries, we collect the content of your messages, email address, and any attachments.
  • User-Generated Content: Any custom cases, notes, theories, or content you create within the App.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with the App, including cases played, time spent, features used, and in-game achievements.
  • Device Information: We collect information about your device including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and visionOS build information.
  • Performance Data: Frame rates, crash reports, error logs, and performance metrics to improve stability.
  • Location Data: We do not collect precise location data, but we may collect general location information (e.g., country/region) for localization purposes.
  • Spatial Data: Information about how you use spatial features, hand gestures, gaze patterns, and eye tracking (limited to interaction optimization, not recording).
  • Analytics: We use analytics tools to understand how users interact with our App, including page views, features used, and user flow.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Apple Services: Information provided through Apple's authentication services and in-app purchase systems.
  • Marketing Partners: Information from third-party advertising networks and analytics providers.
  • Public Sources: Information obtained from publicly available sources to verify user information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Providing Services: To deliver, maintain, and improve the App and our services.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, authenticate users, and provide customer support.
  • Transaction Processing: To process payments and fulfill purchases, subscriptions, and in-app transactions.
  • Personalization: To customize your experience, remember your preferences, and provide recommended cases based on your play history.
  • Analytics and Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, troubleshoot issues, and optimize App performance and features.
  • Communication: To send you service-related announcements, updates, promotional materials, and respond to your inquiries.
  • Safety and Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access; enforce our terms of service.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  • Legitimate Business Interests: To understand user needs, conduct market research, and develop new features.

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, including payment processors, cloud hosting providers, analytics services, and customer support platforms. These providers are contractually obligated to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us.
  • Business Partners: If we conduct joint promotions, marketing campaigns, or partnerships, we may share limited information with our partners with your consent.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, subpoena, or other legal process.
  • Safety and Protection: We may disclose information to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, and that of our users and the public.
  • Business Transfers: If we are acquired, merged, or sell substantially all of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. The retention period may vary depending on the type of information:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal obligations.
  • Payment Information: Retained only for the time necessary to process transactions and comply with tax and accounting regulations (typically 3-7 years).
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely for analytical purposes.
  • Communications: Retained for customer service and dispute resolution purposes, typically 3 years after the last interaction.
  • Support Tickets: Retained for 2 years after resolution.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. We will delete your information within 30 days unless we are required to retain it for legal or operational reasons.

6. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher encryption protocols.
  • Storage Security: Personal information is stored in secure data centers with restricted access, firewalls, and intrusion detection systems.
  • Access Controls: We limit access to personal information to employees, contractors, and service providers who need access to perform their duties. All personnel with access to personal information are subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • Payment Security: Payment processing is conducted through PCI DSS-compliant payment processors. We never store complete credit card numbers.
  • Vulnerability Management: We conduct regular security assessments, penetration testing, and code reviews.
  • Incident Response: We have an incident response plan to address security breaches and notify affected users as required by law.

While we strive to protect your information using reasonable security measures, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security. You use our App at your own risk.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 GDPR Rights (European Residents)

  • Right of Access: You have the right to request a copy of your personal information.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal information in certain circumstances ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request restriction of processing of your information.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your information in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing of your information for marketing or profiling purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent to process your information at any time.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You can file a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

  • Right to Know: You can request what personal information we collect, use, and share.
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of personal information we have collected from you.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt-out of the "sale" of your personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

7.3 Other Rights

  • You can opt-out of marketing communications by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in emails or adjusting your preferences in the App.
  • You can manage cookies and tracking technologies through your browser settings.
  • You can request to opt-out of analytics tracking.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below. We will respond to your request within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for authentication, security, and basic App functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the App to improve performance and functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used by analytics providers to track usage patterns and user behavior.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for targeted advertising and marketing campaigns.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect App functionality. For more information about cookies, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

9. Third-Party Links and Services

Our App and website may contain links to third-party websites and services that are not operated by us. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party services, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services before providing your information.

10. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

We may transfer your personal information to countries other than where you reside, including countries that may not have the same data protection laws. By using our App, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules. When we engage in international data transfers, we implement appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses, to protect your information.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated Privacy Policy on our website and App with a "Last Updated" date.
  • Sending you an email notification for significant changes.
  • Obtaining your explicit consent if required by applicable law.

Your continued use of the App after changes to the Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms. We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
