# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Spatial CRM

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

Spatial CRM transforms customer relationship management from flat databases into living, breathing 3D ecosystems where sales teams can literally walk through customer journeys, manipulate relationship networks with their hands, and collaborate in shared customer spaces. This revolutionary platform enables sales professionals to visualize complex B2B relationships as spatial networks, track deal flow through immersive pipelines, and leverage AI agents that proactively identify opportunities while predicting customer behavior with unprecedented accuracy.

### Strategic Vision
* **Business Transformation**: Evolution from data entry to spatial relationship orchestration
* **Competitive Advantage**: 10x deeper customer insights through 3D visualization
* **AI Integration**: Predictive relationship intelligence and autonomous engagement
