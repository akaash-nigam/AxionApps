# Vision Pro Specific FAQ

## 1. Spatial Computing Value

### Why does this need to be a spatial app vs traditional 2D?
Construction happens in 3D space but is managed through 2D drawings, creating a fundamental disconnect that causes 30% of construction work to be rework. Critical spatial relationships between trades remain invisible until costly conflicts occur on-site. In spatial computing, project managers gain X-ray vision to see through walls and floors, understanding exactly how systems interact before installation. This spatial awareness reduces rework by 75%, prevents 90% of coordination conflicts, and improves safety incident rates by 80% through real-time hazard visualization.

## 2. Comfort & Accessibility

### How do we ensure comfortable extended use?
Construction managers spend entire days on job sites, so the system optimizes for all-day wear in challenging environments. The hybrid reality approach maintains visual connection to the physical site while adding digital overlays, preventing disorientation. Brightness adapts automatically to indoor/outdoor transitions. Voice commands work in noisy environments. The interface remains readable in bright sunlight or dim interiors. Rugged protective cases allow safe use around construction hazards while maintaining comfort.

### What's the minimum and ideal physical space?
The system works anywhere on a construction site - from cramped mechanical rooms to open structural frames. Pass-through video ensures safe navigation while viewing digital overlays. The spatial tracking adapts to GPS outdoors and visual markers indoors. Ideal usage involves walking the site freely while maintaining awareness of physical hazards. Remote managers can virtually visit sites from offices, experiencing the full spatial environment without travel.

## 3. Technical Implementation

### How do we leverage Vision Pro's unique capabilities?
Advanced SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) aligns BIM models to physical structures with millimeter precision. Pass-through video with digital overlay creates X-ray vision effects. Hand tracking enables intuitive measurements and markup directly on structures. Eye tracking identifies what managers focus on, pre-loading relevant documentation. Spatial audio allows communication with specific workers regardless of physical distance on site.

### What are the performance considerations?
The system renders complex BIM models with millions of elements while maintaining 90fps through intelligent occlusion culling and level-of-detail optimization. Edge computing on-site reduces latency for real-time tracking. 5G connectivity enables instant cloud synchronization. Local caching ensures functionality in areas with poor connectivity. The platform handles projects from single homes to massive infrastructure while maintaining smooth performance.
