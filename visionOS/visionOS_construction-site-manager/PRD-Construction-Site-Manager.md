# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Construction Site Manager

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

The Construction Site Manager transforms job site management from clipboards and 2D plans into an immersive 3D command center where project managers walk through virtual construction sites overlaid on reality, track progress in real-time, coordinate crews through spatial awareness, and leverage AI to predict delays, prevent accidents, and optimize workflows. This spatial platform enables construction teams to literally see through walls, visualize future phases, and ensure quality control at every step while reducing waste and improving safety.

### Strategic Vision
* **Business Transformation**: From reactive site management to predictive construction orchestration
* **Competitive Advantage**: 50% reduction in delays, 90% fewer safety incidents
* **AI Integration**: Autonomous progress tracking with predictive optimization
* **Platform Strategy**: Become the spatial nervous system for global construction
