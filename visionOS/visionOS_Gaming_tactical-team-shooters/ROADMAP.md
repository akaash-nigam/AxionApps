## Development Principles

### Core Values

1. **Competitive Integrity**: Fair, skill-based gameplay
2. **Performance First**: Maintain 120 FPS on Vision Pro
3. **Accessibility**: Inclusive for all skill levels
4. **Innovation**: Leverage visionOS unique capabilities

### Release Cadence

- **Major updates**: Quarterly (vX.0.0)
- **Minor updates**: Monthly (vX.X.0)
- **Patches**: As needed (vX.X.X)

### Priorities

1. Performance & stability
2. Competitive balance
3. New content
4. Quality of life improvements

## Metrics & Success Criteria

### Key Performance Indicators

- **Daily Active Users (DAU)**: Target growth
- **Retention**: Day 1, 7, 30 retention rates
- **Match Quality**: Balanced teams, low ping
- **Player Satisfaction**: Store rating > 4.5
- **Esports Adoption**: Tournament participation

### Technical Metrics

- **Performance**: 120 FPS maintained
- **Crash Rate**: < 0.1%
- **Network Quality**: < 50ms average latency
- **Load Times**: < 5s to match start
