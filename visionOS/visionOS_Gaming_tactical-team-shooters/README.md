# Tactical Team Shooters
**Tagline:** "Precision teamwork in full spatial battlefield environments"

## Overview
Tactical Team Shooters reimagines competitive team-based combat by utilizing Vision Pro's spatial capabilities to create realistic battlefield environments that extend around and above players. Teams coordinate complex strategies using real-world movement, spatial communication, and environmental awareness that mirrors actual tactical operations, while maintaining the precise gunplay and strategic depth that defines competitive tactical shooters.

## Core Gameplay Features

### Spatial Tactical Combat
- **360-Degree Battlefields**: Engage enemies from any direction including above and below positions
- **Real Movement Mechanics**: Use actual crouching, leaning, and spatial positioning for tactical advantage
- **Environmental Cover**: Take cover behind real furniture enhanced with virtual battlefield elements
- **Vertical Strategy**: Utilize height differences and multi-level environments for tactical superiority

### Team Coordination Systems
- **Spatial Communication**: Point and gesture-based callouts for precise team coordination
- **Formation Movement**: Teams move through space maintaining tactical formations and positioning
- **Role Specialization**: Support players provide cover while entry fraggers clear spaces systematically
- **Strategy Execution**: Complex multi-phase tactics requiring precise timing and spatial awareness

### Realistic Tactical Elements
- **Ballistics Simulation**: Realistic bullet physics affected by distance, gravity, and environmental factors
- **Equipment Systems**: Tactical gadgets and tools that interact naturally with spatial environments
- **Sound Masking**: Use environmental audio and movement to mask tactical approaches
- **Intelligence Gathering**: Reconnaissance and information sharing through spatial observation
