# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: 2025-11-19

## Introduction

Tactical Team Shooters ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### Account Data

- **Username**: For identification in-game
- **Email**: For account recovery (optional)
- **Password**: Hashed and encrypted

### Gameplay Data

- **Statistics**: Kills, deaths, matches played, win rate
- **Match History**: Recent game results
- **Rank**: Competitive ranking
- **Settings**: Game preferences

### Device Data

- **Device Type**: Apple Vision Pro model
- **OS Version**: visionOS version
- **App Version**: Game version
- **Performance Metrics**: FPS, crashes (anonymous)

### Network Data

- **IP Address**: For matchmaking and server connection
- **Latency**: For optimal server selection
- **Connection Type**: WiFi vs cellular

## What We DON'T Collect

- ❌ Real name
- ❌ Location data (beyond matchmaking region)
- ❌ Payment information (handled by Apple)
- ❌ Biometric data
- ❌ Hand tracking data
- ❌ Eye tracking data
- ❌ Room scanning data

## How We Use Your Data

### Game Functionality

- Match you with other players
- Track your progress and stats
- Maintain leaderboards
- Prevent cheating

### Service Improvement

- Fix bugs and crashes
- Optimize performance
- Balance gameplay

### Communications

- Important service announcements
- Security alerts
- Optional newsletter (opt-in)

## Data Sharing

### We Share With

- **Apple**: For in-app purchases and App Store
- **No one else**: We do not sell or share your data

### We DO NOT

- ❌ Sell your data
- ❌ Share with advertisers
- ❌ Use for targeted advertising
- ❌ Share with third-party analytics

## Your Rights

### Access

Request copy of your data:
- Settings → Privacy → Download My Data

### Deletion

Delete your account and data:
- Settings → Privacy → Delete Account
- Email privacy@tacticalsquad.com

### Correction

Update incorrect data:
- Settings → Profile → Edit

### Opt-Out

Disable optional data collection:
- Settings → Privacy → Analytics (toggle off)
