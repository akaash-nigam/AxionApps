## Extended Description

Tactical Team Shooters is the first competitive tactical shooter built from the ground up for Apple Vision Pro. Leveraging visionOS's spatial computing capabilities, players use natural hand gestures to reload weapons, throw grenades, and switch loadouts, while eye tracking enables precision aiming that feels truly next-generation.

Featuring realistic ballistics with bullet drop and travel time, 5v5 competitive matchmaking with ELO rankings, and spatial audio that lets you hear enemies' exact positions in 3D space, Tactical Team Shooters delivers the intense tactical gameplay you expect with innovations only possible on Vision Pro.

Whether you're a competitive gamer seeking the future of esports or a tactical professional training for real-world scenarios, Tactical Team Shooters offers an unprecedented level of immersion and precision.

## Key Features

- **Revolutionary Controls**: Hand gesture weapon handling and eye tracking aiming
- **Competitive Gameplay**: 5v5 ranked matches with ELO matchmaking
- **Realistic Ballistics**: Bullet drop, travel time, and authentic weapon physics
- **Spatial Audio**: Hear enemies in full 3D with directional sound cues
- **Dynamic Environments**: Room mapping generates cover from your real space
- **Professional Training**: Tactical scenarios for military and law enforcement
- **120 FPS Performance**: Buttery smooth gameplay optimized for Vision Pro
- **Multiple Game Modes**: Bomb Defusal, Team Deathmatch, and more

## Technology Highlights

- Built with Swift 6.0 for modern performance
- RealityKit for stunning 3D graphics
- ARKit spatial tracking and mapping
- Client-side prediction with server reconciliation
- Entity-Component-System architecture
- Optimized for 120 FPS sustained gameplay
