# Financial Trading Cockpit

> Immersive 3D financial trading platform for Apple Vision Pro

## Overview

Financial Trading Cockpit transforms financial market visualization by rendering markets as 3D topographical landscapes, options chains as spatial spirals, and risk exposure as physical barriers. Execute trades through natural gestures and gain unprecedented situational awareness of market dynamics.

## Key Features

- **3D Market Topography** - Markets rendered as dynamic terrain where elevation = price
- **Spatial Options Chains** - Options spiral around underlying stocks in 3D
- **Physical Risk Barriers** - Budget limits appear as walls that prevent overspending
- **News Impact Ripples** - Breaking news propagates visually through correlated securities
- **Gesture-Based Trading** - Execute trades with natural hand gestures
- **Multi-Monitor Workspace** - Infinite spatial canvas for charts and analysis
