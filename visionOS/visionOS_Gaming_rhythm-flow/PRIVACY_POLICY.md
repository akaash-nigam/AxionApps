## Introduction

Welcome to Rhythm Flow! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our visionOS rhythm game.

**Simple Summary**:
- ✅ All data stays on your device
- ✅ No cloud sync, no servers
- ✅ No tracking or analytics (without consent)
- ✅ No ads, no third-party data sharing
- ✅ You control all your data

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

**Gameplay Data**:
- Player profile (username, avatar if selected)
- Game scores and statistics
- Player level and experience points
- Song completion history
- Settings and preferences
- Custom beat maps (if created)

**Fitness Data** (Optional - Fitness Mode Only):
- Workout duration
- Estimated calorie burn
- Movement intensity
- Session history

**Technical Data**:
- Device preferences (graphics settings, audio volume)
- Accessibility settings
- Tutorial completion status

### 2. Data We DO NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:
- ❌ Your name, email, or contact information
- ❌ Your location
- ❌ Your browsing history
- ❌ Your photos or camera access
- ❌ Your contacts
- ❌ Any personally identifiable information (PII) without explicit consent
- ❌ Advertising identifiers
- ❌ Usage analytics (unless you opt in)

---

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Data Usage

All data is used **exclusively** on your device to:

1. **Save Your Progress**
   - Track scores, levels, and unlocked content
   - Remember your preferences and settings

2. **Provide Gameplay Features**
   - Calculate scores and combos
   - Track player statistics
   - Generate personalized challenges

3. **Improve Your Experience**
   - Remember difficulty preferences
   - Adapt gameplay to your skill level
   - Provide accessibility features

4. **Fitness Tracking** (if enabled)
   - Estimate calories burned
   - Track workout sessions
   - Integrate with Apple HealthKit (with permission)

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only

- **All data stored on your device** using iOS secure storage
- **No cloud sync** in current version
- **Encrypted at rest** using iOS data protection
- **Sandboxed** from other apps

### Security Measures

We protect your data through:
- ✅ iOS Data Protection API
- ✅ Keychain for sensitive data
- ✅ App sandboxing
- ✅ No network transmission (except multiplayer, if enabled)
- ✅ Regular security reviews

## Health Data (HealthKit Integration)

### What We Access

If you enable Fitness Mode and grant HealthKit permission:
- We **write** calorie and workout data to HealthKit
- We **DO NOT read** any health data
- You control what's shared via iOS Settings

### Your Control

- Enable/disable at any time in Settings
- Delete health data from Health app
- Revoke permission in iOS Settings → Privacy → Health
