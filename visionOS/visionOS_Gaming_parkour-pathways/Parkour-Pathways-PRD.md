# Parkour Pathways - Product Requirements Document
*Spatial Gaming in the AI Era*

## Section 1: Game Concept & Abstract

Parkour Pathways converts any space into a dynamic parkour training ground by overlaying virtual obstacles, challenges, and movement paths throughout your environment. Using Vision Pro's advanced spatial awareness capabilities, players navigate complex obstacle courses that adapt to their physical space, learn real parkour techniques through guided instruction from professional athletes, and compete in time trials that push both physical and mental agility to new limits, revolutionizing how movement training and spatial fitness intersect.

### Core Elements
* **Genre & Setting**: Adaptive movement training and competitive spatial fitness with parkour focus
* **Unique Spatial Hook**: Any indoor space becomes unlimited parkour training environment with AI-generated obstacle courses
* **AI Integration**: Intelligent course generation, real-time movement analysis, personalized training programs, safety monitoring
* **Target Platform**: Leverages Vision Pro's spatial mapping, motion tracking, and real-time environmental adaptation
