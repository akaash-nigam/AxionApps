## Information We Collect

### Data You Create

When you use Reality Annotations, you create content including:

- **Annotations:** Text, images, audio recordings, and 3D models you attach to spatial locations
- **Layers:** Organization structures you create for your annotations
- **Spatial Data:** 3D position, orientation, and scale information for where you place annotations
- **Metadata:** Creation dates, modification dates, and annotation properties

**Storage:** All this data is stored locally on your Apple Vision Pro device using SwiftData.

**Your Control:** You have complete control over this data. You can view, edit, or delete any annotation at any time.

### Automatic Technical Data

The App automatically collects minimal technical information necessary for functionality:

- **AR Tracking Data:** Temporary spatial mapping data used by ARKit to position annotations in your environment. This data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted.
- **Device Information:** Device model, visionOS version (used only for app compatibility, stored locally)

**Important:** This technical data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

### Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

- ‚ùå No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
- ‚ùå No location data (GPS coordinates, addresses)
- ‚ùå No usage analytics or statistics
- ‚ùå No crash reports (unless you explicitly opt-in via Apple)
- ‚ùå No device identifiers for tracking
- ‚ùå No behavioral data or profiling
- ‚ùå No photos or camera feeds (beyond AR processing)
- ‚ùå No microphone recordings (except annotations you create)
- ‚ùå No contact information or address book access

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Processing

All data processing happens locally on your Apple Vision Pro device:

1. **Annotation Creation:** When you create annotations, the content is processed and stored locally using SwiftData
2. **AR Rendering:** Spatial data is used to render annotations in the correct physical location
3. **Organization:** Layer information is used to organize and filter your annotations
4. **Search:** Text content is indexed locally for fast searching

### iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable iCloud sync:

- **Storage:** Your annotations are synced to your personal iCloud account
- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Apple's iCloud encryption
- **Apple's Control:** iCloud data is controlled by Apple, subject to Apple's iCloud Terms and Privacy Policy
- **Our Access:** We have zero access to your iCloud data

**You Control Sync:** You can enable or disable iCloud sync at any time in the App settings.

### No Server-Side Processing

Reality Annotations does not have backend servers that store, process, or analyze your data. All functionality is client-side.

## Data Security

### Local Security

Your data on your device is protected by:
- **Device Encryption:** visionOS encrypts all data at rest
- **Secure Storage:** SwiftData uses iOS secure storage mechanisms
- **No Network Transmission:** Data never leaves your device (except iCloud sync)

### iCloud Security

If you use iCloud sync:
- **End-to-End Encryption:** Your data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- **At-Rest Encryption:** Apple encrypts data stored in iCloud
- **Authentication:** Requires your Apple ID credentials
- **Two-Factor Authentication:** Supported and recommended

### App Security

- ‚úÖ No third-party SDKs or frameworks that could access your data
- ‚úÖ No analytics or tracking code
- ‚úÖ Minimal permissions (only camera for AR tracking)
- ‚úÖ Regular security updates
- ‚úÖ Code reviewed for vulnerabilities

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access Your Data

You have complete access to all your data:
- View all annotations in the App
- Export annotations (future feature)
- Access via iCloud.com (if sync enabled)

### Modify Your Data

You can:
- Edit any annotation at any time
- Update layers and organization
- Change annotation properties

### Delete Your Data

You can delete your data at any time:

**Delete Individual Annotations:**
1. Open annotation detail view
2. Tap Delete button
3. Confirm deletion

**Delete All Local Data:**
1. Delete the Reality Annotations app
2. All local data is permanently removed

**Delete iCloud Data:**
1. Go to iOS Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Manage Storage
2. Select Reality Annotations
3. Delete data
OR
4. Disable iCloud sync in app settings
5. Delete all annotations
6. Re-enable sync (will push empty state)

### Control iCloud Sync

You can control sync:
- Enable/disable at any time in App settings
- Choose what syncs via iOS Settings
- Delete cloud data independently of local data

### California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident:
- **Right to Know:** You have the right to know what personal information we collect (None)
- **Right to Delete:** You have the right to delete your data (Delete app or annotations)
- **Right to Opt-Out:** You have the right to opt-out of sale (We don't sell data)
- **Non-Discrimination:** We won't discriminate for exercising your rights

### European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA:
- **Right to Access:** You can access all your data in the app
- **Right to Rectification:** You can edit your data anytime
- **Right to Erasure:** You can delete your data anytime
- **Right to Data Portability:** Export feature coming soon
- **Right to Object:** You can object to processing (N/A - no processing)
- **Legal Basis:** Consent and contract performance

**Data Controller:** You are the data controller of your annotations. We are merely a software provider.
