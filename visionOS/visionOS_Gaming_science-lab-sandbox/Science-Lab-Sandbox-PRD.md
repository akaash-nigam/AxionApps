# Science Lab Sandbox - Product Requirements Document
*Spatial Gaming in the AI Era*

## Section 1: Game Concept & Abstract

Science Lab Sandbox transforms any space into a fully-equipped scientific laboratory where players conduct realistic experiments across chemistry, physics, biology, and astronomy. Using Vision Pro's spatial precision, students and enthusiasts safely perform dangerous experiments, manipulate molecular structures, explore quantum mechanics, and discover scientific principles through hands-on experimentation that would be impossible or unsafe in traditional laboratory settings. This revolutionary educational platform makes advanced scientific research accessible while maintaining authentic experimental methodology and safety protocols.

### Core Elements
* **Genre & Setting**: Educational simulation and scientific experimentation with comprehensive laboratory access
* **Unique Spatial Hook**: Unlimited laboratory space with dangerous experiments conducted safely
* **AI Integration**: Realistic scientific simulation, intelligent tutoring, collaborative research guidance
* **Target Platform**: Utilizes Vision Pro's precision tracking, spatial manipulation, and educational presentation
