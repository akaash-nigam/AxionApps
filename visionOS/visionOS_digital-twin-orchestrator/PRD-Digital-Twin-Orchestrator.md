# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Digital Twin Orchestrator

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

The Digital Twin Orchestrator creates living, breathing 3D replicas of physical assets, processes, and entire facilities that operations teams can explore, manipulate, and optimize in real-time. This revolutionary platform enables engineers and operators to step inside virtual factories, walk through digital power plants, and predict equipment failures before they occur, all while AI agents continuously optimize performance and predict future states with unprecedented accuracy.

### Strategic Vision
* **Business Transformation**: From reactive maintenance to predictive optimization
* **Competitive Advantage**: 100% visibility into physical operations digitally
* **AI Integration**: Self-optimizing digital twins with predictive intelligence
* **Platform Strategy**: Become the mirror world for all physical operations
