# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: November 24, 2025

Language Immersion Rooms ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our visionOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**:
- User ID (via Sign in with Apple)
- Email address (optional, via Sign in with Apple)
- Username (chosen by you)

**Learning Data**:
- Vocabulary words encountered
- Conversation history (stored locally)
- Learning progress and statistics
- Study streaks and milestones

### Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data**:
- App features used
- Session duration
- Error logs and crash reports (anonymized)

**Device Information**:
- Device type (Apple Vision Pro)
- Operating system version (visionOS)
- App version

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:
- ❌ Precise location data
- ❌ Contacts or address book
- ❌ Photos or videos
- ❌ Financial information
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Personal conversations (stored locally only)
- ❌ Voice recordings (processed on-device, not stored)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

**Provide the Service**:
- Create and manage your account
- Track learning progress
- Sync data across devices (optional)
- Provide personalized learning experiences

**Improve the Service**:
- Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
- Fix bugs and errors
- Develop new features
- Improve AI responses

**Communicate With You**:
- Send important updates
- Respond to support requests
- Notify about new features

We do NOT use your information for:
- ❌ Advertising
- ❌ Selling to third parties
- ❌ Profiling for marketing
- ❌ Any purpose not listed above

## How We Share Your Information

### Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

**OpenAI** (AI Conversations):
- Purpose: Power AI conversation partners
- Data Shared: Your Spanish messages during conversations
- Privacy: Sent via encrypted API, not used for training
- Policy: [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy/)

**Apple Services**:
- Sign in with Apple: Authentication
- CloudKit: Optional data sync (encrypted)
- Speech Recognition: On-device processing
- Privacy: [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/)

### We Never Share

We will NEVER:
- ❌ Sell your personal information
- ❌ Share data with advertisers
- ❌ Use data for unrelated purposes
- ❌ Share without your consent (except legal requirements)

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal process
- Protect our rights or property
- Prevent fraud or abuse
- Protect user safety

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Data is Stored

- **On-Device**: Conversation history, progress data
- **iCloud** (Optional): Encrypted backup and sync
- **Our Servers**: Minimal (User ID, subscription status only)

### How We Protect Data

- ✅ End-to-end encryption for cloud sync
- ✅ Secure API connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- ✅ No plain-text storage of sensitive data
- ✅ Regular security audits
- ✅ Access controls and monitoring

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data kept while you use the app
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data erased within 30 days

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control

You have the right to:

**Access**: Request a copy of your data
- Email: privacy@languageimmersionrooms.com
- Response time: Within 30 days

**Correct**: Update inaccurate information
- Available in app settings

**Delete**: Request deletion of your data
- Settings → Account → Delete Account
- Or email: privacy@languageimmersionrooms.com

**Export**: Download your learning data
- Settings → Account → Export Data

**Opt-Out**: Disable optional features
- Analytics: Settings → Privacy → Analytics
- CloudKit Sync: Settings → Account → iCloud Sync

### California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination

**We do not sell personal information.**

### European Residents (GDPR)

EU/EEA residents have additional rights:
- Right to data portability
- Right to restriction of processing
- Right to object to processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authority

**Legal Basis**: Consent and legitimate interests
