# Surgical Training Universe
*Immersive Medical Education and Surgical Simulation*

## Executive Summary

Surgical Training Universe transforms medical education and surgical training through immersive spatial computing experiences that provide unprecedented realism, precision, and accessibility. Built for Apple Vision Pro, this comprehensive platform enables medical students, residents, and practicing surgeons to train in risk-free virtual environments that replicate real surgical scenarios with anatomical accuracy and haptic feedback.

## Problem/Solution

**Problem**: Traditional surgical training relies on limited cadaver access, expensive physical simulators, and high-stakes learning environments where mistakes can have serious consequences. Medical institutions struggle with standardized training delivery, skill assessment, and providing adequate hands-on experience for all learners.

**Solution**: Surgical Training Universe creates unlimited access to diverse surgical scenarios in immersive 3D environments where learners can practice procedures repeatedly, receive instant feedback, and develop expertise without risk to patients. Transform surgical education from resource-constrained training to unlimited immersive practice.

### Running the Project

**Prerequisites**:
- macOS 14.0+
- Xcode 16.0+ with visionOS SDK
- Apple Vision Pro or visionOS Simulator

**Build and Run**:
```bash
# Open project
cd SurgicalTrainingUniverse
open SurgicalTrainingUniverse.xcodeproj

# Run tests
xcodebuild test -scheme SurgicalTrainingUniverse \
  -destination 'platform=visionOS Simulator,name=Apple Vision Pro' \
  -enableCodeCoverage YES

# Build for device
xcodebuild -scheme SurgicalTrainingUniverse \
  -destination 'platform=visionOS' build
```
