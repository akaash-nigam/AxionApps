# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Surgical Training Universe

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

The Surgical Training Universe transforms medical education from cadavers and observation into immersive operating theaters where surgeons perform thousands of procedures on virtual patients, feel tissue resistance through haptic feedback, collaborate with global experts in real-time, and leverage AI to achieve surgical mastery without risking a single life. This spatial platform enables surgeons to reduce training time by 60%, achieve competency 5x faster, and master rare procedures that save lives when seconds count.

### Strategic Vision
* **Medical Transformation**: From "see one, do one, teach one" to "do thousands, perfect one"
* **Clinical Advantage**: 5x faster surgical competency with zero patient risk
* **AI Integration**: Adaptive surgical coaching with predictive complication prevention
* **Platform Strategy**: Become the spatial operating room for global surgical excellence
