## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I change tiers later?**
A: Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on next billing cycle.

A: No setup fees for standard subscriptions. Implementation packages are optional.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: Yes, pause for up to 3 months (data retained). Contact support to pause.

**Q: What's included in "unlimited stores"?**
A: No hard limit, but fair use policy applies (contact for very large deployments).
