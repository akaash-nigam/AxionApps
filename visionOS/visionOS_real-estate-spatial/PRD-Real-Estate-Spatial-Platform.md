# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Real Estate Spatial Platform

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

The Real Estate Spatial Platform transforms property viewing and management from static photos and rushed visits into immersive spatial experiences where buyers walk through photorealistic properties from anywhere, sellers stage homes with virtual furniture, agents host global open houses, and AI predicts buyer preferences to match perfect properties instantly. This spatial platform enables real estate professionals to reduce showing time by 70%, increase qualified leads by 300%, and close deals 40% faster through immersive property experiences that transcend physical limitations.
