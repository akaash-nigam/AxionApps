# Hide and Seek Evolved
**Tagline:** "The ultimate game of stealth in augmented reality"

## Overview
Hide and Seek Evolved transforms the classic childhood game into a sophisticated spatial gaming experience where players use real furniture, virtual camouflage, and advanced stealth mechanics to hide from seekers who must locate them using environmental clues, spatial reasoning, and detective skills. The game leverages Vision Pro's spatial mapping to create hiding opportunities that blend real and virtual elements seamlessly.

## Core Gameplay Features

### Advanced Hiding Mechanics
- **Virtual Camouflage**: Blend into backgrounds using digital camouflage that adapts to your environment
- **Furniture Integration**: Hide behind, under, and inside real furniture enhanced with virtual concealment options
- **Size Manipulation**: Temporarily shrink to hide in unexpected places or grow to intimidate seekers
- **Environmental Mastery**: Use room lighting, shadows, and sound masking for strategic concealment

### Intelligent Seeking Systems
- **Clue Detection**: Find virtual footprints, disturbances, and environmental evidence
- **Sound Tracking**: Use spatial audio to locate hidden players through breathing and movement
- **Thermal Vision**: Temporarily reveal heat signatures to aid in player detection
- **Time Pressure**: Limited seeking time creates tension and strategic decision-making

### Dynamic Environment Interaction
- **Furniture Rearrangement**: Move virtual objects to create new hiding spots or block access
- **Room Transformation**: Environments change between rounds to create fresh hiding opportunities
- **Weather Effects**: Virtual rain, fog, or darkness affect visibility and hiding strategies
- **Multi-Level Play**: Utilize vertical space including virtual platforms and hiding spots

## Technical Innovation

### Spatial Stealth Technology
- **Occlusion Detection**: System accurately determines what players can and cannot see
- **Movement Tracking**: Precise detection of player position and movement for fair gameplay
- **Sound Masking**: Realistic audio occlusion based on room geometry and furniture placement
- **Line-of-Sight Calculation**: Advanced algorithms determine visibility between players

### Adaptive Environment System
- **Real-Time Mapping**: Continuously updates hiding opportunities as room layout changes
- **Safety Boundaries**: Ensures all hiding spots are safe and accessible for all players
- **Fairness Algorithms**: Balances hiding opportunities to ensure competitive gameplay
- **Environmental Storytelling**: Creates narrative context for different hiding scenarios
