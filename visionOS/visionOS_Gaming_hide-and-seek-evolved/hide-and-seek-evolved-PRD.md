## Section 1: Game Concept & Abstract

Hide and Seek Evolved transforms the classic childhood game into a sophisticated spatial gaming experience where players use real furniture enhanced with virtual camouflage to hide from seekers who must locate them using environmental clues, spatial reasoning, and advanced detective mechanics. The game leverages Vision Pro's spatial mapping to create hiding opportunities that seamlessly blend physical and digital elements, while AI dynamically adapts the environment and balances gameplay fairness. Players experience the thrill of stealth through size manipulation, environmental mastery, and intelligent seeking systems that create tension and strategic depth impossible in traditional hide and seek.

### Core Elements
* **Genre & Setting**: Family-friendly stealth game combining physical activity with spatial augmented reality
* **Unique Spatial Hook**: Real furniture integration with virtual enhancement and spatial tracking impossible on traditional platforms
* **AI Integration**: Dynamic environment adaptation, fair gameplay balancing, and intelligent seeking assistance
* **Target Platform**: Vision Pro spatial mapping, occlusion detection, and multiplayer tracking systems

## Appendix B: Gesture & Interaction Library

### Hiding Gestures
```
Enter Hiding Mode: Palm to face (quiet mode)
Size Manipulation: Two-hand spread/compress motion
Camouflage Activation: Slow waving motion for virtual concealment
Ready Signal: Thumbs up when positioned and ready
Emergency Exit: Both hands raised above head
```

### Seeking Gestures
```
Point to Search: Index finger pointing for location examination
Thermal Vision: Circle around eye with index finger
```
