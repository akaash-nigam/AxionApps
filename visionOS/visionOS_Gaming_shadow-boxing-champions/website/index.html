Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need boxing experience?

Not at all! Shadow Boxing Champions is designed for everyone from complete beginners to professional boxers. Our tutorial will teach you the fundamentals, and the AI difficulty scales to your skill level.

How much space do I need?

Minimum 2m √ó 2m (6.5ft √ó 6.5ft) of clear space. We recommend 3m √ó 3m for the full experience with complete freedom of movement.

Will I really get a good workout?

Absolutely! Users typically burn 200-400 calories per 30-minute session. Our fitness workouts are designed by professional trainers to maximize cardio and strength benefits while maintaining proper boxing form.

Does it work with Apple Health?

Yes! Shadow Boxing Champions integrates with HealthKit to track workouts, calories burned, heart rate, and more. All your boxing sessions appear in your Apple Health dashboard.

Can I play multiplayer?

Yes, with Champions Pass! Compete in live tournaments, challenge friends, and climb global leaderboards. We use skill-based matchmaking to ensure fair, competitive matches.

What if I get motion sickness?

Our game is designed with comfort first. There's no artificial movement or camera rotation - you're always in control. We also offer comfort mode with reduced visual effects if needed.
