# AI-Era Spatial Enterprise PRD: Industrial Safety Simulator

## Section 1: Executive Summary & Spatial Vision

The Industrial Safety Simulator transforms workplace safety training from classroom lectures and static videos into immersive life-or-death scenarios where workers experience hazards without risk, practice emergency responses until they're automatic, understand consequences viscerally, and leverage AI to predict and prevent accidents before they happen. This spatial platform enables organizations to reduce workplace incidents by 90%, save lives, avoid millions in costs, and create a culture where safety becomes instinctive rather than enforced.
